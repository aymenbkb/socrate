About EDtech Group

A Registered Dedicated to Equitable Education

EDtech Group is a registered EdTech charity working to close the global digital divide. We design open, accessible technologies and community programs that help learners in underserved communities access skills, verified credentials, and lifelong learning pathways.

Our work combines research, partnership with schools and NGOs, and people‑centered design to ensure learning tools respect privacy, promote inclusivity, and increase opportunities for learners everywhere.
